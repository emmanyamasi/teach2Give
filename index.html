FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

+

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend skills. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

+

Yes, Frontend Mentor has free challenges, but there are also premium options with additional features.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

+

Yes, you can use the completed projects in your portfolio to showcase your skills.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?

+

You can join the Frontend Mentor community or ask for help on the Slack group or forums.
